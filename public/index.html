Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect the bot to my WhatsApp?
Enter your phone number in the connection form above, generate a pairing code, then go to WhatsApp Settings → Connected Devices → Connect a Device → Pair with Phone Number and enter the generated code.
Is the bot safe and secure to use?
Yes, our bot uses the official WhatsApp Multi-Device API with end-to-end encryption. We don't store personal information and follow strict privacy guidelines to ensure your data remains secure.
What commands are available with the bot?
Type ".menu" in any chat to see all available commands. Features include media downloads, AI chat, group management, translations, weather updates, news, memes, and much more.
Can I use the bot in group chats?
Absolutely! The bot works perfectly in both private and group chats, with special group management features like member tagging, admin tools, auto-moderation, and welcome messages.
Is there any cost to use the bot?
The core features are completely free to use. We may introduce premium features in the future, but essential functionality will always remain free for everyone.
How do I get support if I have issues?
Join our WhatsApp support group or contact us directly through the social links below. Our dedicated team is available 24/7 to help with any questions or technical issues.
Does the bot work on multiple devices?
Yes! Thanks to WhatsApp's Multi-Device API, the bot works seamlessly across all your connected devices without requiring your phone to be online.
Can I customize the bot's responses?
Yes, you can create custom commands, set personalized auto-responses, configure welcome messages, and tailor the bot's behavior to match your specific needs and preferences.
